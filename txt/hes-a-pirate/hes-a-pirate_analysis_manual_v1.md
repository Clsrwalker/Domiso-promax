# Analysis (Manual Musician v1): hes-a-pirate.mid

## Song Character
- iconic action-theme motif with repeated rhythmic cells and rising heroic gestures
- listener focus should be on attack, momentum, and clear thematic contour

## Manual Style Decision
- best fit is **cinematic swashbuckling orchestral** style, not soft ballad and not pure literal stack
- preserve propulsion with strong bass ostinato and articulated harmonic pushes

## Layer Decisions
- Voice A: from restore pass to keep main motif readable and singable at high tempo
- Voice B: from longnote pass for stronger forward harmonic drive in mid-layer
- Voice C: from restore pass to keep punchy low-end rhythmic anchors

## Intended Result
- clearer heroic theme compared to fully dense stacking
- keeps excitement while reducing mud at fast BPM
