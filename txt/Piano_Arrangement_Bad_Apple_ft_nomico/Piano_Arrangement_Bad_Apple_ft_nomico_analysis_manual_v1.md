# Analysis (Manual Musician v1): Piano_Arrangement_Bad_Apple_ft_nomico.mid

## Song Character
- signature identity comes from repeating dark motif, steady machine-like pulse, and long-form dynamic build
- over-legato treatment weakens drive; over-stacked harmony quickly gets muddy on limited-key layout

## Manual Style Decision
- chose **dark pulse synth-rock** style: motif-first, pulse-second
- preserved repetition and attack clarity instead of adding decorative notes

## Layer Decisions
- Voice A: from restore_dense for clearer hook motif articulation and phrase tracking
- Voice B: from guitar_dense for motoric rhythmic comping and section propulsion
- Voice C: from restore_dense for rigid low anchor and stable harmonic floor

## Intended Result
- more recognizable Bad Apple groove pattern at first listen
- energetic but controlled density, avoiding ornament-heavy blur
