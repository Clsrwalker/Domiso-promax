# Analysis (Manual Musician v2): the-voice-tada-koe-hitotsu-rokudenashi.mid

## Song-Oriented Style Decision
- this piece behaves like a vocal-led cinematic pop ballad rather than a pure dense piano etude
- best listening result on DoMiSo is **lead-first with sectional accompaniment depth**, not maximal simultaneous notes

## Arrangement Decisions
- Voice A: kept from restore pass to preserve singable contour and phrase identity
- Voice B: switched to longnote-style support to avoid masking lead in verse/pre-chorus
- Voice C: switched to longnote-style bass movement for steadier harmonic floor and breathing space

## Why This Is Different From Script-Only
- not chosen by one fixed script profile; layers were selected by musical role per section
- avoids both extremes: over-thinned (too empty) and over-literal (too crowded)
