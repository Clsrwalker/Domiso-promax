# Analysis (Manual Musician v1): the-rumbling-by-sim-from-attack-on-titan.mid

## Song Character
- the track's identity is chant-like lead motifs over heavy, machine-like low-frequency pounding
- too sparse loses pressure; too dense obscures chant articulation and impact

## Manual Style Decision
- chose **industrial march-metal** style for punch and controlled aggression
- kept lead slogans front, with rhythm layer acting as propulsion not ornament

## Layer Decisions
- Voice A: from restore_strict to keep main chant motifs direct and recognizable
- Voice B: from guitar_dense to inject chugging rhythmic drive and section lift
- Voice C: from restore_strict for pounding repeated bass spine

## Intended Result
- stronger Rumbling character and momentum at first listen
- clearer lead under heavy texture, without mud build-up
