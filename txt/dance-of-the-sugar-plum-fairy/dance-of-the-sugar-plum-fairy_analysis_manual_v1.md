# Analysis (Manual Musician v1): dance-of-the-sugar-plum-fairy.mid

## Song Character
- iconic character comes from dry staccato motif, toy-like celesta attack, and steady low pulse
- too legato makes it lose identity; too dense harmony turns the texture muddy and less "dancing"

## Manual Style Decision
- chose **crisp celesta dance** style for articulation-first recognizability
- kept primary motif sharply exposed, with controlled mid-layer color only

## Layer Decisions
- Voice A: from restore_strict to keep recognizable short-note melodic motif and phrasing
- Voice B: from longnote script to add eerie middle texture without replacing the lead role
- Voice C: from restore_strict for dry, repetitive bass pulse and clear harmonic grounding

## Intended Result
- preserves the signature "sugar plum" contour and rhythm at first listen
- richer than strict-only output, but avoids over-stacked clutter
