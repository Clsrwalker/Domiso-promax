# Rush E Real Manual V1

## Goal
- Keep the piece recognizable on 21-key DoMiSo instead of chasing the full impossible piano wall.
- Preserve four things only: the opening hook, the first rush-theme burst, the absurd chord-wall gesture, and the tail punchline.

## Why Script Versions Break Down
- The source has `46291` notes, extreme polyphony, and thousands of micro-tempo points.
- The late sections are visually dense but not musically legible after 21-key snapping and octave folding.
- Full restoration creates token noise faster than it creates recognizability.

## Manual Strategy
- Intro: keep the dry repeated-note joke exactly, because that is the identity anchor.
- Rush-theme section: take the strongest melodic contour from the script highlight output and leave room around it.
- Chord-wall section: keep one representative impossible block from the dense arranged output instead of the whole wall.
- Tail: keep the accelerating high-register taunt and abrupt cadence.

## What Was Intentionally Removed
- Late-stage note-wall windows where the top line disappears under constant collisions.
- Most micro-tempo jitter.
- Decorative filler that only makes the result sound like parser spam.

## Result
- The manual TXT is a condensed performance version, not a full-score transcription.
- It should sound more like "Rush E simplified for DoMiSo" and less like a failed literal import.
