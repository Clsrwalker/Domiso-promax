# Rush E Real Manual V2

## Direction Change
- V1 was a condensed performance cut.
- V2 is a full-structure manual adaptation: intro, build, central rush, impossible-wall section, tail cycle, and coda are all present.

## Manual Reduction Rules
- Keep the recognizable contour whenever a dense source passage still has a singable top line.
- Convert unreadable note-walls into repeated harmonic cells and bass ostinato instead of deleting the section.
- Replace source micro-tempo jitter with a few structural tempo zones so the piece still feels like one continuous performance.

## What Stayed
- Opening repeated-note joke.
- Main scalar rush gestures.
- Mid-song "absurd" chord-wall identity.
- Ending taunt/cadence section.

## What Changed
- The most extreme late walls were reduced into motif loops, not literal note spam.
- Harmony and bass were made more repetitive on purpose so the listener can still track the form.
- This version is still simplified, but it is no longer a fragment cut.
